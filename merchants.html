Purple Mintows cost approximately 1 US Cent per coin, at the most. If you buy in different packages, you will be paying a bit less per coin. The more you buy, the less you pay per coin. The cost also varies from country to country. In some countries they are a bit more expensive and in some slightly cheaper.

How do you work out the cost of Purple Mintows in different countries?

Very complex economic indicators, currency exchange rates and a host of other mind boggling factors are used to determine the cost of Purple Mintows in each country.

Can I buy Mintows at another country's price, while I am visiting another country?

No, the Mintows will be priced based on which country the merchant is registered in. But you can buy Mintows from anywhere, as long as you can go online and use your credit card.

Why do you say Purple Mintows, are there other types of Mintows?

Yes, there are Gold Mintows too, but Merchants use only Purple Mintows.

Are the Purple Mintows transferable?

No, they are not transferable, neither between users, nor between merchants. They can only be transferred by a merchant to a user, and can be zapped from the user's account by the same merchant who gave him the Mintows, in the first place, in exchange of some goodies.

Mintows works on a system of building long term relationships with customers, which we call the Customer Relationship Initiative or CRI. Many such single persons add up to generate a large volume of consistent sales over a period of time, than one flash deal at one point of time. Mintows is technology centric, designed to be a continuous bridge between the customers and the merchants, which develops into a very personal realtionship.
