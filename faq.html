FAQ

FAQ

I have 2 phone connections; can I sign up from each of them separately?

No, each account is linked to an individual.

How can I accumulate the Gold Mintows?

Gold Mintows can be accumulated by registering on Mintows, promoting and using the app and participating in activities.

How many Gold Mintows would I get when my friends register?

You will get 10 Gold Mintows per friend, when they register, provided you are the first one to recommend them. If you are not the first, you will only get 1 gold mintow when they register.

What can I get with the Gold Mintows?

All the items in the Mintows Store.

How can I redeem the Gold Mintows?

The Gold Mintows can be redeemed by exchanging them against things at the Mintows Store.

Do I have to collect minimum number of Gold Mintows before I can redeem them?

No, there is no limit to the number of Mintows to be collected before redeeming it.

How can I get more Gold Mintows once I have exhausted what I have?

There are 2 ways of doing it. You can either earn or buy them.

What happens if I want to buy something from the Mintows Store and I do not have enough Mintows?

You can either earn or buy them.

How can I earn Gold Mintows?

You can earn Gold Mintows by participating in tasks or activities on the app.

How do I know when I get Gold Mintows?

You will get a notification in the app and you would also receive an email.

Do I get 10 Mintows Coins when I recommend or when my friends register?

You will get 10 Gold Mintows for each of your friends who register.

Is the QR code specific to mobile numbers?

No, the QR code is not specific to mobile numbers; they are specific to the person signing up.

Would this work only when I have a Wi-Fi connection?

No, it will also work with 3G and 2G.

Q14. If I buy things at a store 3 times, making different purchases, can I collect Purple Mintows 3 times?

Yes, you can do that. Every time you buy, you can collect Mintows at the time of payment.

How many Purple Mintows do I need to have to be able to redeem them?

It depends on the rules set by the merchant. You would normally be able to see the Mintows being given away when you see the promotion.

How do I know how many Gold and Purple Mintows I have collected?

Ans. It will be kept on record in MY MINTOWS, you can check it there.

Are both Gold and Purple coins called Mintows?

Yes, they are but to be specific we also mention the colour of the Mintows. This whole program is also called Mintows, as well.

If I do not use this app for a considerable time, do I have to re-register?

No, it’s a one time registration.

How long can I remain inactive or not make any purchase?

You can remain inactive for as long as you want, however, the already collected Gold and Purple Mintows in your account may expire and cannot be used. But you can continue to collect fresh Mintows and use them.

How does my account get cancelled?

The account does not get cancelled; unles you specifically de-register from the application.

Do the Gold and Purple Mintows come with an expiration date, if so what date?

Yes, they do in some cases. It depends on the rules set by the merchants, for Purple Mintows. For Gold Mintows the rules are set by the Mintows application. You will be intimated by email, well in advance before the expiry of your Mintows.

Can 2 or more people club their Purple Mintows and redeem them together?

No, that can not be done.

Are Purple Mintows interchangeable between merchants?

No, they are not. You will have to redeem the Mintows in the specific outlet you have got them from.

Would the deals be for restaurants and pubs as well?

The deals are open to all kind of businesses.

Do I need an internet connection to see the deals and collect the Mintows?

Yes, you do.

If I want to use the app abroad, do I have to register again?

No, you don’t have to register again. it remains accessible anytime anywhere.

Can I go to a different country and avail Mintows offers?

Yes, you can, as long as Mintows has Merchants there.

How many Purple Mintows do I get per purchase?

The number of Purple Mintows to be given per purchase will be decided by the merchant.

What kinds of merchants are enlisted in your site?

We have no restrictions on who can be a Mintows merchant. Any merchant can be a Mintows merchant.

If my friend and I refer the same person and that person registers. Who gets the Gold Mintows and how many?

Both of you would get Gold Mintows. However, the one who recommends this person first would get 10 Gold Mintows and the other one would get 1 Gold Mintows.
