Welcome to Mintows

 

With Mintows you get listings and push notifications on deals and promos being offered by Merchants around you, that help you decide on what to buy and where to buy from.

Registration is a piece of cake with your Facebook or Google account and you can instantly start getting location based market updates. There is no need to fill up long forms or remember elusive passwords. Once you register, you get 1000 Gold Mintows. For each of your friends who register, you get 10 Gold Mintows.

Every user on Mintows gets a unique QR code, which you can scan at a Mintows Merchant and collect Purple coins. You can then redeem these Purple coins at the merchant where you collected them by again, simply scanning your QR code. No coupons, no pocket full of plastic cards, just you and your smartphone.

You can redeem your Gold Mintows for goodies at the online Mintows store, where there are hundreds of useful and fun items to choose from.

Its Fast, Free, Fun, Secure, Simple and Rewarding. Get Mintows, and become a happy shopper.

HOW IT WORKS

1) Register directly or via you Facebook or Google account and get your unique QR code.
2) Collect 1000 Gold Mintows upon registration
3) Recommend to your friends and collect 10 Mintows each time a friend registers on Mintows.
4) Allow your device to get location based updates on your device from Mintows Merchants.
5) Whenever you make a purchase at a Mintows Merchant, scan your QR code and collect Purple Mintows.
6) When you have a fistful of Purple Mintows, exchange them at the Merchant where you got them for goodies offered by the Merchant, by scanning your QR code again.
7) Earn more Gold Mintows by continuing to use the Mintows app and interacting with it.
8) Exchange your Gold Mintows for goodies at the Mintows store.

Mintows are coins you collect by using the Mintows App – they come in two types, Gold and Purple.

Mintows brings you information about stores, promos, deals, products and generally what is hot and happening in your locality and in your vicinity, wherever you are at the time, to help you make a smart decision about what to buy and from where. It let's you share the amazing deals and steals that have made your day; with your friends on Facebook or Google Plus, and Tweet your ratings of a particular deal.

The coins can be redeemed for goodies once you have a fistful of Mintows.
