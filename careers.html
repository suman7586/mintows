About

Mintows is a start­up with a great potential, and we are looking for bright people to are passionate about creating Happy Shoppers. Engineers make up 75% of our company—so if you like writing code and have great ideas to make shopping a happy and rewarding experience,write to us.

Interface Engineer

If you enjoy writing code for mobile phones and are passionate about User Experience......

Job Responsibilities:

  • Create new, awesome features used by Mintows users.
  • Ability to work in a very small team.
  • Own release and QA cycles of your code.

Job Requirements:

  • BS in computer science or equivalent.
  • Strong Java or Obj­C programming and debugging skills
  • Strong C/C++ programming and debugging skills
  • Experience developing native mobile apps is strongly preferred, but not required
  • Startup mindset
  • We are also looking for summer interns!

Customer Support

If you are looking for a fun job, have a smart phone, consider yourself a smartphone gadget geek, and believe that smartphones will change the way we interact with the world around us........

Job Responsibilities:

  • Provide customer support via Email to Mintows users and Merchants.
  • Interface with developers and report bugs
  • Update and evolve product documentation and other marketing text

Job Requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree or equivalent.
  • Ability to write competently in English and know your spellings and grammar
  • Know the difference between GSM and CDMA, Android and IOS, Nokia and Windows
  • Passionate about mobile devices and applications
  • Fluent in English

  • Software Engineer

  • Job Requirements:

    Ability to cook up a good idea and execute it in a running program. Background or knowledge:

    • Mobile application development process
    • Software development for Mobile Apps
    • Application architecture, object oriented design, multi­threaded programming

  • Why work here:

    • Potentilly Profitable
    • Small and idea driven product
    • Flexitime
    • Competitive salary and compensation packages
    • Full health benefits
